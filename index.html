How it works

Start small. It gets better naturally as your history grows.

1) Add a receipt (or a total)

Enter grocery receipt items, or log a dining out meal with just the final total.

2) Create meals

Pick ingredients, set servings, and MealCost calculates cost per meal and cost per person.

3) Watch trends

Use your charts to spot what’s rising, what’s stable, and what’s worth adjusting.

About MealCost

MealCost was built for a simple reason: food costs are hard to “feel” until you can see them. Instead of guessing, MealCost helps you capture what you actually spent, then turns it into cost-per-meal numbers and grocery price trends over time. It’s designed to be quick to use, easy to understand, and useful even if you only track a few meals each week.
