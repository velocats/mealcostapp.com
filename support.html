Quick FAQs

Common questions we see.

Does MealCost require an account?

No — MealCost does not require an account.

Where is my data stored?

Your meal, item, and cost data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, it’s stored in your personal iCloud account.

Do you track me or run ads?

No — MealCost does not use advertising, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection services.

Do you upload my receipt images?

Only if you use the Receipt Import feature. When you import a receipt, the image is sent to a secure proxy server, then to Mindee (OCR) and Anthropic (Claude) for processing. Images are used only to extract item names and prices and are not stored or used for any other purpose.

Do you share or sell data?

No — MealCost does not sell, share, or disclose user data to third parties beyond what is required to process receipt imports (see above).

How do I request a feature?

Email what you’re trying to do and what you expected to happen. Short is fine.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

MealCost helps users track the cost of meals and food spending.

Data Storage

MealCost stores meal, item, and cost data locally on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.

Data Collection

MealCost does not collect personal information, does not require an account, and does not transmit user data to external servers.

Camera and Photos

MealCost may access the camera or photo library to scan or import receipts. If you use the Receipt Import feature, receipt images are transmitted to a Cloudflare-hosted proxy server for processing. That server sends the image to Mindee for optical character recognition (OCR) and to Anthropic (Claude) for item name expansion and food classification. Images are used only to extract receipt data and are not stored beyond the duration of the request.

Third-Party Services

Receipt Import uses the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — secure proxy server infrastructure
  • Mindee — optical character recognition (OCR) for receipt parsing
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI-assisted item name expansion and classification

These services receive only the receipt image and return structured data (item names and prices). No personal information is sent. Each service's own privacy policy applies to their processing.

Tracking and Advertising

MealCost does not use advertising, analytics tracking, or third-party data collection services.

Data Sharing

MealCost does not sell, share, or disclose user data to third parties, except as described above for receipt image processing.
