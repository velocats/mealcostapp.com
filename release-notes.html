MealCost Release Notes

Version 1.1.1

This update introduces Taxes Paid tracking. You can now enter sales tax on both grocery receipts and dining out meals, and taxes are included in total meal and receipt costs.

A new Taxes Paid chart has been added to the Insights tab. View tax totals by week, month, or year and toggle between All, Grocery, or Eating Out to better understand where your tax spending is coming from.

Dining Out meal details now display tax separately, receipt totals correctly include tax, and sample data has been updated with realistic tax amounts for improved insights.
