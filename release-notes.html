Release Notes

What’s new in MealCost. Short, scannable updates you can check before or after upgrading.

v1.1.1

February 2026

New Taxes Paid tracking and Insights chart.

Taxes Paid Tracking

  • You can now enter Sales Tax on grocery receipts.
  • You can now enter Sales Tax on dining out meals.
  • Taxes are included in total meal cost calculations.

New Taxes Paid Chart

  • Added a dedicated Taxes Paid section in Insights.
  • View tax totals by Week, Month, or Year.
  • Toggle between All, Grocery, or Eating Out.
  • All mode displays stacked bars to show category breakdown.
  • Summary values update dynamically based on selected mode.

Sample Data Improvements

  • Sample receipts and meals now include realistic Sales Tax values.
  • Standardized sample tax rate for consistency across charts.
