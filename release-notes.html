v3.0

May 2026

Receipt Import — photograph a grocery receipt and import items automatically. Review and edit everything before it saves.

New: Receipt Import

  • Photograph a grocery receipt using your camera or choose one from your photo library.
  • MealCost extracts item names, prices, tax, and discounts automatically.
  • Review and edit everything on a dedicated screen before anything is saved.
  • 5 free Receipt Imports included. Additional packs available as a one-time purchase.

Food vs Non-Food Classification

  • Items are automatically classified as food or non-food during import.
  • Non-food items (paper towels, cleaning supplies, etc.) are flagged and excluded from grocery totals.
  • Tap the pill on any item to toggle its classification before or after saving.

New: Savings & Discounts Tracking

  • Discounts and savings lines from scanned receipts are now tracked separately.
  • New Savings & Discounts chart in Insights shows total saved by store.
  • Filter by Month, Year, or All Time. Monthly trend chart for longer periods.

Insights Improvements

  • Charts are now collapsible — tap any section header to expand or collapse it.
  • New setting: include or exclude non-food items from grocery totals and charts.
  • Collapsed state is remembered between sessions.

Polish and Fixes

  • Receipt dates now respect your device locale and timezone correctly.
  • Improved item name and price editing on the receipt review screen.
  • Swipe-to-delete fixed on receipt items.
  • General performance and stability improvements.
